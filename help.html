How To Play

For the basic rules of shogi and how pieces move, see https://lishogi.org/learn or https://shogi-international.com/rules/. The pieces also have an icon on them that indicate how they move if you need a reminder.


In real time Shogi, the base game remains the same; piece movements, drops, and promotions remain unchanged. However, there are no turns. Each player can move any of their available piece at any time.

After a piece has been moved, it will enter a cool-down phase for a short period of time (5 seconds) and may not be moved for that duration. Other pieces not on cool-down can still be moved.

Dropped pieces will also start with a cool-down immediately after being dropped to the board.


Once you connect to a game, click the button that says "Click when ready". When both players are ready, the game will start.
